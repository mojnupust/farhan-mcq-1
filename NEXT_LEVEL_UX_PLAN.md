## 🎯 Phase 1: Conversion & First Impressions (রেভিনিউতে সরাসরি প্রভাব)

| # | Improvement | Business Impact |
|---|-------------|-----------------|
| 1 | **Social Proof Counter (Live)** — Replace static "৪৫+ শিক্ষার্থী" with a real-time animated counter that ticks up | Builds trust → more sign-ups |
| 2 | **Free Trial Gate** — Add a "Try 5 Free MCQs" flow before requiring sign-up (guide: "দিনে ৫টি প্র্যাকটিস") | Reduces sign-up friction → more conversions |
| 3 | **WhatsApp CTA Floating Button** — Persistent bottom-right floating WhatsApp button for instant support/payment queries | Direct channel for payment → faster revenue |
| 4 | **Pricing Toggle (Monthly/Quarterly/Annual)** — Show all 4 packages (Free/Basic/Premium/Unlimited) from guide instead of just ১৯০/month | Upsell opportunity → higher ARPU |
| 5 | **Countdown/Urgency Banner** — "প্রথম ১০০ জনকে ৫০% ডিসকাউন্ট" with countdown timer | Creates FOMO → faster decisions |

## 🎯 Phase 2: Engagement & Retention (Churn কমানো)

| # | Improvement | Business Impact |
|---|-------------|-----------------|
| 6 | **Streak System** — Show daily practice streak on dashboard with fire 🔥 emoji, celebrate milestones | Habit formation → lower churn rate |
| 7 | **Progress Dashboard** — Visual progress bars per category (e.g., "জব সলিউশন: ৪৫/১৫০ MCQ done") | Gamification → more engagement |

| 9 | **Push Notification Prompts** — "আজ আপনার Daily MCQ বাকি আছে!" with gentle reminders | Re-engagement → retention |
| 10 | **Exam Result Sharing** — "Share your score" to WhatsApp/Facebook with branded card | Organic marketing → free referrals |

## 📱 Mobile-First Priorities (টার্গেট অডিয়েন্সের জন্য)

আপনার টার্গেট অডিয়েন্স (সরকারি চাকরি প্রার্থী, বাংলাদেশ) প্রধানত ব্যবহার করেন:
- Budget Android phones (ছোট স্ক্রিন)
- Slow 3G/4G connections
- bKash/Nagad for payment

**Critical mobile UX improvements:**

1. **Reduce landing page weight** — lazy load below-fold sections
2. **Add offline support** — Service Worker for cached MCQs
3. **Touch-optimized exam interface** — large tap targets for MCQ options (min 48px)
4. **Bottom sheet for payment instructions** — instead of navigating away
5. **Haptic feedback on answer selection** — if browser supports it
